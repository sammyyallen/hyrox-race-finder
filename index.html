How this works: A scraper runs every 15 minutes, fetching ticket category data from HYROX's official vivenu storefronts (gb.hyrox.com, usa.hyrox.com etc.) — the same data your browser loads when you visit the buy page. It writes availability.json, which this page reads on load. Green = tickets available now. Amber = ≤20 spots remaining. Red = sold out. Grey = division is offered but availability not yet fetched (event not on sale yet, or scraper hasn't reached it). Prices shown are live from vivenu where available. Always confirm on hyrox.com before purchasing. Unofficial fan tool — not affiliated with HYROX GmbH.
